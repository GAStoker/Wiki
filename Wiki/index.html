Survival


The definition of survival is:"The state or fact of continuing to live or exist, typically in spite of an accident, ordeal, or difficult circumstances." There is many ways to survive each situation is different and every choice is the difference between life and death. Everything you do effects your physical health well being and more important your mental health. Your most important tool is your mind without it you can not survive.
